AuraChess

OVERVIEW

Aura Chess is a visual tool designed to help you develop chess tactics by showing exactly which squares are being dominated by which player. Rather than just showing legal moves, it maps board control and piece influence to help you quickly identify underlying strategies.

launch AuraChess

01 // HOVER_ANALYTICS

Hover Coverage Diagnostics: Hover over any active square to see a small badge (e.g., (W2|B3)) that displays exactly how many White vs. Black pieces are currently attacking or defending that specific square.

Winning Square Overlays: Evaluates total board control. Squares are softly highlighted with a translucent Blue overlay if White has more pieces covering them, or Red if Black has the numerical advantage. Ties remain naturally blank.

02 // DEEP_ANALYSIS

Comprehensive X-Ray: Visualizes true "battery" potential. Sliding pieces (Queens, Rooks, Bishops) will intelligently show their influence through blocking pieces that share the same trajectory.

Filter Skewers / Pins: If a piece is pinned or skewered between an enemy attacker and a friendly piece of strictly HIGHER value, all of its coverage vectors are physically severed from the board math because moving it would be a blunder.

03 // GRANULAR_CONTROL

Source Pieces Filter: Uncheck specific pieces in the sidebar to instantly strip their influence from the total calculations, letting you isolate specific tactical setups.

Target Squares Minimap: Use the interactive 8x8 minimap to focus strictly on certain areas of the board. Only squares toggled ON (Green) will participate in the analytics.

04 // MOVEMENT_LOGIC

You can drag-and-drop pieces or click squares to make standard chess moves. The board records your history natively, meaning you can easily use your keyboard's Left Arrow and Right Arrow keys to step backward and forward through your gameplay.
