Proliphia

OVERVIEW

Proliphia is an AI-based learning and idea development tool that I am building to help me learn more efficiently, synthesize information across subjects, and generate stronger ideas over time.

01 // SYSTEM_WORKFLOW

The system combines conversation, teaching, documentation, and scheduled recall into a single workflow. When I learn something new or explore an idea in conversation with Proliphia, the system can generate structured notes from that discussion, connect those notes to my existing knowledge base, and schedule future review sessions to reinforce understanding. This makes it possible to revisit important concepts, refine ideas, and synthesize connections between topics rather than starting from scratch each time.

02 // AUTOMATED_DOCUMENTATION

The system also includes automated documentation. After a conversation with Proliphia, it can generate a structured record of what was discussed, including key ideas, questions, and insights. These records become part of a growing knowledge base that can be revisited, expanded, and integrated with future learning.

03 // VAULT_INTEGRATION

A key feature of the system is its integration with my Obsidian vault. During conversations, Proliphia can reference my prior notes and use them as context when explaining new ideas or answering questions. This allows learning to build directly on earlier work and helps maintain continuity across projects and subjects.

04 // RECALL_SYSTEM

Another central component is the recall system, which is designed to support long-term memory. When I study a topic, Proliphia can automatically schedule follow-up sessions at intervals such as two weeks, one month, or three months. During these sessions, the system prompts questions about the material, encourages reflection, and helps move knowledge from short-term understanding into long-term memory.
