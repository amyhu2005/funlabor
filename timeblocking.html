TimeBlock

OVERVIEW

TimeBlock is a simple visualization tool I built to better understand how I spend my time and to see hours invested into skills accumulate over time. Instead of tracking time in lists or spreadsheets, the app represents hours as blocks on a grid, making progress immediately visible and easy to reflect on.

LAUNCH_TIMEBLOCK

01 // GRID_INTERACTION

Create Subjects: Define categories such as studying, exercise, or personal projects.

Log Hours on the Grid: Click on a subject and place blocks on the grid to record time spent.

Edit Time Blocks: Blocks can be updated or removed at any time to keep records accurate.

02 // TREND_ANALYSIS

View Subject Trends: Click a block to see time patterns for a specific subject.

The system automatically generates trend summaries to help identify peaks in productivity and skill investment across different days.

03 // AUDIT_&_SOCIAL

Time Audit: Include activities like sleep to understand how many hours remain available.

Share or Export Data: Save progress as an image or share logs with others to maintain accountability.
